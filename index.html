MY STORY

Below are some of the details about Richy

  • Richy born December 21, 1989, in Kampala,
    Uganda is a singer-songwriter
    • .
    Richy has enjoyed tremendous success on both the Reggae and Dancehall music charts. He is a highly versatile dancehall reggae artiste who performs four different genres of music.
    Richy grew up in Kampala, where he was raised by his father and mother, and began singing in church as a child. The vocal talents of Richy have been noted since his humble beginnings. Richy was inspired to become a musician by the songs he used to listen to in his father?s car. Among others, Richy's parents have been the source of his motivation in pursuing his musical ambitions. However, it was in High School that his love for music was developed and nurtured.His popularity and musical talent has attracted collaborations from other artistes such as Konshens, Radio and weasel , AY, EKKY, Lilian Mbabazi, Don Mc, Cindy, Shaydee,Iyaya,Karl Wolf among othersÉ.. Richy has also written songs for artists such as Bebe Cool, Lilian Mbabazi(Blue3),Jackie Kyandilu(Blue3)Cindy(Blue3),Ekky Among Others Richy has ranked up awards, garnering HiPipo Awards Nominations for: (Best Dancehall and Breakthrough Artist 2014) and winning the Buzz Teeniez Awards Best Breakthrough and Dancehall Artist 2014), Zzina Awards (Best Breakthrough Artist), Best Of The Best Awards (Best Breakthrough Artist 2014) Uganda Entertainment Awards (Best Dancehall Artist 2015) Rising Star Awards (Breakthrough Artist 2015) HiPipo Awards (Best Zouk Song 2016) Buzz Teeniez Awards (Best Male Artist, Best Collaboration , Best Riddim 2016). Richy states, “It has been a wonderful journey so far, filled with new and promising encounters. It is also challenging but I love what I am doing, so it makes it that much easier.” My main objective is to take music to the optimum level. “I want every household across the world to know and love my music and where I come from”, Richy enthuses. Whether it is personal or musical, I just think I’m a walking art piece, just a ball of creativity. Currently Richy is the most sought after brand, with association with corporate brands such as Buzz, Ambition Mission (a project that involved sensitizing the youth about the dangers of smoking tobbaco and also encouraging students to stay in school and have ambitions and goals in life), He has worked on several projects with Airtel Uganda; Happiness continues, Kyabiise, Tugabane AND He is currently working on a campaign with KFC Uganda.
